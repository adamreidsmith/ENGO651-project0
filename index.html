About
I graduated with a BSc Honors in Mathematical Physics from the University of Alberta in 2020, and I am currently a MSc student in the Department of Geomatics Engineering at the University of Calgary. At the U of A, I was involved in several research projects, including the simulation of an ultrasensitive screener for radioactive surface contamination, and the design and implementation of a machine learning algorithm for generation of artificial optical data for the PICO-60 dark matter search experiment. My current research interests are in machine learning and quantum computing, specifically their applications in geomatics engineering.
