Sport
Outside of academics, athletics and fitness play a major role in my life. During my undergraduate degree, I competed on the U of A rowing team and raced in several national level regattas. Following a prominent foot injury in 2021, I took up trail and mountain running as my primary sport. Since then, I have competed in several trail races ranging from 3 km ascent races to 50 km, and have aspirations to continue training for longer races in the years to come. I spend as much time as possible in the Rockies, either mountain running and climbing in the summer, or nordic and downhill skiing in the winter. I love pushing myself on technical, steep terrain and long outings with lots of vertical. Check out my Strava profile for a more detailed view of my athletic endeavours.
